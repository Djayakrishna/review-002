Tour package 2 : Arrival - Dubai

Day 01: Arrival - Dubai

On your arrival at Sharjah Airport, our tour representative will meet and greet you. He will ensure your transfer to the Fortune Plaza hotel for check-in. Relax and enjoy the hotel hospitality. Later in the evening, you will proceed to the Marina cruise for an unforgettable experience of the Dhow Cruise where a relaxed ambience and spectacular views await under the beaming moonlight. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 02: City tour and Burj Khalifa

After a hearty morning breakfast at the hotel, our representative will take you on the Dubai city tour The tour guide will start with instructions about the day’s itinerary and offer detailed information about the city’s main landmarks as you pass along the route. The tour will proceed to the famous Burj Khalifa, the tallest man-made structure in the world. You will take the “At the Top” tour to experience the tower’s modern architectural beauty and head to the observation deck 124 floors above to take in the spectacular 360-degree aerial view of the city, desert, and ocean below. Enjoy the fountain show when the colourful and illuminated fountain jets sway to a variety of melodies, leaving you spellbound. After an exciting day, proceed to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 03: Desert Safari

Start your day with a leisurely breakfast at the hotel. In the afternoon, your guide will take you to the Desert Safari. Here, an experienced safari driver will give you a thrilling ride through the exciting desert by driving in the dunes along with some dune bashing. Enjoy a range of adventurous activities at the desert camp, from camel rides and stargazing to sandboarding, sand-skiing, and a hot air balloon ride.

Day 04: Dubai Miracle Garden and Dubai Frame

Post your breakfast; the guide will come for hotel pick up. You will head to the Dubai Miracle Garden, the worlds most extensive natural flower garden spread over 72,000 square metres with over 50 million flowers and 250 million plants. The Dubai Butterfly Garden is located close by and has a climate-controlled dome, providing the butterflies with a relaxed environment. The garden features 15,000 butterflies of around over 50 varieties of butterflies in different sizes and colours.

Day 05: Dubai and Meena Bazaar and Gold Souk

Post your breakfast; the tour guide will pick you up from the hotel for a trip to Dubais famous shopping haven, Meena Bazaar. Named after one of the first shops in the area, Meena Bazaar is now known for its various fabric shops. Hundreds of shops sell raw silks, wool, silk, pashmina, cashmere, and cotton fabrics. Many shops display outfits for special occasions like weddings and festivals. Apart from textiles, shops sell shoes, bags, ceramics, perfumes, spices, and jewellery.

Tour package 3:Singapore Tour

Day 1: Sentosa Island Tour

After you land in Singapore, our tour representatives will greet you at the airport and take you to your hotel. Once you have checked in and freshened up, it is time to begin your fabulous Singapore vacation.

Day 2: Gardens by the Bay - National Gallery Singapore - Merlion Park - Singapore Zoo

The first stop on your second day is Gardens By The Bay, a nature park that covers an area of 101 hectares and has three waterfront gardens After this, head to the National Gallery Museum, which displays mesmerizing pieces of art from the Southeast Asian community.

Day 3: Universal Studios - Chinatown

You must visit Universal Studios theme park in Singapore, the first of its kind in Southeast Asia. Don’t forget to ride Battlestar Galactica, the world’s tallest duelling rollercoaster Kids and adults will surely enjoy revisiting classic movies such as Transformers, The Mummy, and Jurassic Park. Gift your family a memorable day through a tour of the enchanting Universal Studios. In the evening, head to Chinatown to buy souvenirs for yourself and your loved ones and to relish some delicious oriental cuisine.

Tour package 4:Japan, Tokyo

Day 1 : Arrive Tokyo

After your arrival at Narita, our tour representatives will meet you and get transferred to the hotel by bus. You can enjoy your overnight stay at Tokyo.

Day 2 : Tokyo City tour

Take your breakfast in the morning. Get ready for a guided sightseeing tour to Tokyo. You will be taken to Tokyo Tower, Akasaka Guest House, Meiji Shrine, National Diet Building and so on. Continue your journey towards the Imperial Palace East Garden, Imperial Palace Plaza, Tasaki Pearl Gallery, Ginza Shopping District, AsakUsa Kannon temple, Sumida River Cruise and Nakamise shopping street. On this day, start an early tour to Mt. Fuji and visit the Fuji-Izu-Hakone National Park and the Owakudani Volcano Valley. Check over the Peace Pagoda and the Golden Buddha in the Hakone Peace Park. On the way, visit the Crystal Factory with our tour guide. Transfer to the hotel in Mr. Fuji and enjoy a typical Japanese Steamboat dinner with your beloved ones.

Tour package 5:Bali, Kintamani, Ubud, Tanjung Benoa

Day 1 : Bounty Sunset Cruise

Once you arrive in Bali, our local representative will greet you at the airport and take you to your hotel. After having breakfast, proceed to the Benoa Port to board the Bounty Sunset Cruise to enjoy the beautiful turquoise waters and relish some delicious food with your family or partner while watching the beautiful Balinese artists entertain you with dance and music. Get back to the hotel after a fulfilling dinner on the cruise.

Day 2 : Celuk Village - Mas Village - Elephant Cave - Kintamani - Ubud Village Tour

Visit the Mas Village to watch the local woodcarvers create world-famous wooden sculptures, followed by Celuk, a Balinese village reputed for its gold and silver jewelry. The famous Elephant Cave, or Goa Gajah, is the next stop, a revered Hindu archaeological site.

Day 3 : Tanjung Benoa Beach

How can your trip to Bali be complete without witnessing the serene and stunning beaches? Head to Tanjung Benoa Beach, where you can stroll at a leisurely pace, immersing yourself in sunbathing and beachy activities. You may also indulge in water sports and choose from some of the activities included in our package: parasailing, banana boat, and jet skiing. Pamper yourself in the afternoon with two hours of a rejuvenating spa treatment. Spend the night at the hotel.

After your morning breakfast, prepare for a full day tour. This tour will enable you to watch out the beautiful mountain surroundings of Almaty city. Our tour representative will stop at Medeo Gorge where you can view the wonderful mountains of Trans-Ili Ala-Tau and get stunned. Proceed to the Chimbulak Ski Resort (Cable Car Free not included) located at an altitude of 2230 metres above sea level. Enjoy your overnight stay at hotel.
